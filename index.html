--- layout: default --- David Bäcker is a curator and art historian based in Münster, Germany. His work focuses on contemporary art and on interdisciplinary formats at the intersection of visual and performative practices. He has worked with museums, galleries, and cultural festivals on the development and realization of exhibitions and artistic projects. His practice combines curatorial research and exhibition-making with experience in project organization, production, and art handling. Bäcker studied art history in Berlin and Tel Aviv.
